## /tower/sustainability (TowerSustainability)

### Plan — ADD content (currently very sparse):

**1. The Desert Wall story:**
- South wall geometry responds to sun path
- Zero south-facing offices = reduced cooling load
- Deep angled windows minimise direct radiation
- Stone facade absorbs less heat than glass at same surface area

**2. The Engineering Sustainability story:**
- Concrete supertall = lower embodied carbon than steel alternative
- Parametric 3D modeling of all structural elements
- Rotational construction corrections = reduced material waste
- 100% redundancy of power supply
- Smart building: fibre optic backbone + advanced BAS

**3. Specs to add:**
- Insulated glass: low emissivity coating
- 30% of glass units are curved (reducing curtain wall thermal bridging)
